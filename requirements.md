# Bharat AI Sathi
AI Powered Government Scheme Assistant

## 1. Problem Statement

Citizens in India often struggle to:
- Find relevant government schemes.
- Understand eligibility criteria.
- Navigate complex government portals.
- Identify benefits applicable to them.

Existing portals are information-heavy but not user-friendly or intelligent.

## 2. Proposed Solution

Bharat AI Sathi is an AI-powered assistant that:
- Organizes government schemes into structured categories.
- Provides simplified explanations.
- Displays eligibility, benefits, and helpline details.
- Assists users in identifying suitable schemes.

It acts as a centralized and intelligent scheme discovery platform.

## 3. Objectives

- Simplify access to government welfare information.
- Improve awareness of schemes.
- Reduce dependency on middlemen.
- Provide AI-driven assistance for scheme discovery.

## 4. Target Users

- Rural citizens
- Farmers
- Students
- Senior citizens
- Women beneficiaries
- Small business owners

## 5. Functional Requirements

- Display scheme categories.
- List schemes under each category.
- Show scheme details:
  - Objective
  - Benefits
  - Eligibility
  - Required documents
  - Helpline numbers
- Clean and responsive UI.
- AI-based recommendation (future enhancement).

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements

- Fast response time.
- Mobile-friendly interface.
- Scalable architecture.
- Easy data updates via JSON.

- Backend: Python (Flask)
